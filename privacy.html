Privacy Policy

At Yuwi Garden Designs, your privacy is paramount. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect your data. By visiting our website, engaging with our services, or otherwise interacting with us, you agree to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Identification Information

When you contact us, request a quote, book a consultation, or subscribe to newsletters, we may collect personal information that includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name
  • Contact details such as email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Project specifications or preferences
  • Billing and payment information

1.2 Non-Personal Identification Information

We may collect non-personal information automatically when you visit our website, such as browser type, operating system, IP address, pages visited, and browsing behavior.

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze site usage, and improve our services. Cookies help us remember your preferences and tailor content accordingly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We utilize the information collected for a variety of purposes, including:

  • Responding promptly and effectively to your inquiries and service requests
  • Providing and managing landscaping design services tailored to your needs
  • Processing payments securely and maintaining transaction records
  • Sending newsletters, promotional materials, and updates about our services (only if you have opted in)
  • Improving our website’s usability, content, and performance
  • Ensuring security and preventing fraud or unauthorized activities
  • Complying with legal obligations and industry standards

3. Sharing Your Information

Yuwi Garden Designs respects your privacy and does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business, such as payment processors, marketing partners, or IT service companies. These third parties are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and use it solely for the purposes for which it was disclosed.

Additionally, we may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect our rights and property.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your personal information. Our website is hosted on secure servers, and we implement industry-standard security measures such as SSL encryption, firewalls, and access controls to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

While we strive to ensure your data is secure, no electronic transmission over the internet or storage method can guarantee 100% security. We recommend exercising caution when sharing sensitive information online.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access and Correction: You may request access to the personal data we hold about you and ask for any inaccuracies to be corrected.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions included in emails or contacting us directly.
  • Data Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations or legitimate business purposes that require retention.
  • Cookies: You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect your experience on our website.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our services and website are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent. If you believe that we may have inadvertently collected data from a child under 16, please contact us immediately to request deletion.

7. Third-Party Websites and Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites for convenience and informational purposes. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Yuwi Garden Designs reserves the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy on our website, with an updated “last revised” date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
